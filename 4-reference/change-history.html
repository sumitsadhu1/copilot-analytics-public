Evidence Policy

  1. Use current Microsoft Learn pages and official documentation linked from Learn as technical authority.
  2. Record the exact article title, URL, last-updated date, supporting section, and retrieval/validation date for volatile claims.
  3. Surface conflicts between official pages; do not silently choose one. Use conservative wording and require tenant validation or Microsoft Support where needed.
  4. Label author frameworks, recommendations, tenant observations, previews, and temporary limitations separately from Microsoft-documented behavior.
  5. Validate exact product, role, setting, condition, action, portal, and field names before publication.

Publication Gates

  • Technical SME approval for all material product claims and procedures.
  • Security/privacy veto for any unremediated negative test or unsupported security boundary.
  • Independent procedure test for destructive, access-broadening, privacy, partition, and data-source changes.
  • Accessibility validation of HTML and generated PDFs; structural checks do not by themselves prove WCAG or PDF/UA conformance.
  • All HTML and PDFs carry the same release and validation date.
