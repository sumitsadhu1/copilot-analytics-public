Multi-Agency Architecture Decision
Answer three questions to find the right architecture for your tenant

Two architectures exist for deploying Copilot analytics across multiple agencies in a shared M365 tenant: Default Priority (agencies see their own data by default, cross-agency visibility is acceptable) and Isolation (hard data boundaries via partitions).
